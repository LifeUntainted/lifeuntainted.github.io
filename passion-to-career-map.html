Passion to Career Map

Explore your passions and learn how to align them with potential career paths. Use this map to brainstorm, visualize, and take the first step toward a fulfilling career journey.

Identify Your Passions

  • What activities make you lose track of time?
  • What are your hobbies or interests?
  • What problems do you love solving?
  • What would you do if money was not an issue?

Map Your Skills

  • What skills do you have that relate to your passions?
  • Are you knowledgeable about any specific fields?
  • What can you offer that others may need or find valuable?

Discover Career Possibilities

  • Research industries or roles that align with your passions and skills.
  • Look into roles that require a combination of skills you already have.
  • Consider whether you can monetize your passion through freelancing, entrepreneurship, or employment.
