Passion to Career Map

Explore your passions and learn how to align them with potential career paths. Use this map to brainstorm, visualize, and take the first step toward a fulfilling career journey.

Identify Your Passions

Questions to Guide You

  • What activities make you lose track of time?
  • What are your hobbies or interests?
  • What problems do you love solving?
  • What would you do if money was not an issue?
  • What did you love doing as a child?
  • What makes you feel most accomplished?
  • Which activities give you the most energy?
  • What topics could you talk about for hours?
  • What do people come to you for help with?
  • What do you find yourself daydreaming about?
  • What kind of work feels meaningful to you?
  • If you could volunteer for any cause, what would it be?
  • What type of environment do you thrive in?
  • Which books or documentaries do you find fascinating?
